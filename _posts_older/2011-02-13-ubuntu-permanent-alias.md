To create an alias permanently add the alias to your .bashrc file
  
`gedit ~/.bashrc`
  
And then add your alias at the bottom.

Now execute
  
`. ~/.bashrc`
  
in your terminal (there should be a gap between the . and ~/.bashrc.)
  
Now you can check your alias.
