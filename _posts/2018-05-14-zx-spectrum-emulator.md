## Spectrum

	sudo apt install fuse-emulator-gtk spectrum-roms
    fuse-gtk
    # or better
    fuse-sdl BruceLee.tzx.zip
    
In 'Options/peripherals/General' enable 'Kempston joystick' and disable the rest.
