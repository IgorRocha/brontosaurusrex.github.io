how to copy paste in nano

a. line by line

<pre>alt+6   &lt; copy line (copy as many lines as you like, and they will be pasted in the order you copied them)
ctrl+u  &lt;
</pre>

b. select, copy paste

<pre>alt+m+a &lt; select start?
alt+6   &lt; copy
ctrl+u  &lt; paste
</pre>
